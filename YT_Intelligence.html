Capital expenditure can create defensibility if ROIC is high. Best moats combine capital investment with proprietary knowledge (recipes, processes, patents, brand, skills).

Higher gross margins enable better employee compensation, faster cash conversion cycles, and reinvestment capacity. Commodities have low margins; information, media, software, and community have high margins.
